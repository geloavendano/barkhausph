Ch 1

Getting in & finding your way

Logging in, what's on screen, and the two ideas that shape everything: the branch switcher and live updates.

1.1 Logging in

The admin portal lives at barkhaus.ph/admin/. There is no password — you sign in with your Google account 1. Access is controlled by an allow-list: only Google accounts that have been registered as admins can get in.

📷01-login-gate.pngDesktop 1280
Capture: the Gate screen, logged out — Barkhaus logo, "Admin Portal", and the "Sign in with Google" button.
  • 1 Sign in with Google button
If you sign in and land back on this screen, your Google account isn't on the admin list yet. Ask the owner/manager to add your email to the admin users table — then sign in again.

1.2 The screen layout (desktop)

Once in, you'll see three zones: the top bar, the navigation, and the page content.

  • 1 Branch switcher — Estancia / Eastwood. This is the most important control in the app: every page shows only the selected branch's data.
  • 2 Greeting ("Hi, <name>") confirming who is signed in.
  • 3 Sign out (↪) — always sign out on shared devices.
  • 4 Sidebar navigation — Calendar, Bookings, Check In, Members, Inventory.

1.3 The screen layout (mobile)

On a phone, the sidebar disappears. Navigation moves to a bottom bar with four buttons — Calendar, Bookings, Check In, and More 1. Tapping More slides up a small sheet with Members and Inventory 2. The yellow ＋ button (FAB) floats above the bottom bar 3 — that's where you create bookings (Chapter 5).

1.4 Switching branches

Tap the other branch name in the top bar. The calendar jumps back to today on the "All" tab, and the bookings list, check-in queue, members coverage, and inventory all reload for that branch. If something you expect to see is "missing", the selected branch is the first thing to check.

📷04-branch-switch.pngDesktop 1280
Capture: close crop of the top bar with Eastwood selected (highlighted tab) and Estancia unselected.
  • 1 Active branch tab (highlighted border)

1.5 The app keeps itself fresh

You normally don't need to reload anything. The Calendar and Bookings pages update themselves three ways:

  • Instantly when a booking is created or changed anywhere (including a customer booking online) — via a live connection;
  • a safety refresh every minute;
  • an immediate refresh whenever you return to the tab.

You can also press R to refresh on demand, and Esc closes whatever panel or drawer is open. (Full list in Appendix C.)

Ch 2

The Calendar — your home screen

The Calendar shows one day at a time as a 9 AM – 8 PM timeline. Every card is a booking; every hatched stripe is blocked time.

2.1 Reading the day view

  • 1 Booking card — pet name with 🐶/🐱, plus the room/package and owner when the card is tall enough.
  • 2 Card color = resource color: each room and groomer has its own color (set in Inventory), so you can tell at a glance who/where a booking belongs.
  • 3 Status dot — small dot on each card showing booking status (legend in Appendix A).
  • 4 ★ gold star — the booking has a member discount.
  • 5 Red "now" line — current time, only shown when viewing today.
  • 6 Cancelled bookings appear greyed-out with strikethrough.

Ch 3

Finding a booking

The Bookings page is the searchable history of everything ever booked at this branch — newest first, grouped by the day the booking was made.

3.1 The list

Bookings are grouped under date headers 1 (the date the booking was created, not the appointment date). Tap a header to collapse/expand a day. Each row shows: status dot · service badge · reference number · pet & owner · schedule · total 2. Cancelled rows are greyed with a struck-through reference 3.

📷12-bookings-list.pngDesktop 1280
Capture: Bookings page with 2–3 date groups, mixed services, one cancelled row.
  • 1 Date group header with count
  • 2 One row, label each column
  • 3 Cancelled (struck-through) row

3.2 Filter by service

The chips at the top — All · Grooming · Hotel · Daycare · Studio — reload the list for one service. Switching back to All restores everything.

3.3 Search

Type at least 2 characters into the search box 1 to search by:

  • Reference number — e.g. 919A finds BH-919A52
  • Owner first or last name
  • Owner email (partial is fine)
  • Pet name

Results replace the list with a count line 2; clear with ✕ 3 to return to the normal list.

Search one name at a time. Ana or Reyes both work — Ana Reyes (full name) returns nothing.

Inside a booking — the drawer

Click any booking — on the Calendar, the Bookings list, or the Check In page — and the same drawer opens. Everything about one booking lives here.

4.1 Top to bottom tour

  • 1 Reference number + when and how it was booked (Online / Admin / Walk-in).
  • 2 ✏ Edit booking — opens the full edit wizard (Chapter 6).
  • 3 Pet header — name, breed, size, sex, age; ★ if member-discounted.
  • 4 Status pills — booking status and payment status at a glance.
  • 5 Medical notes — red box, impossible to miss. Read it before handling the pet.
  • 6 Temperament & vaccines — declared vaccines show ✓/✕ per item. If the owner uploaded vaccine records online, the files are listed here too.

Ch 5

Creating a booking

Customers normally book online, but the front desk creates bookings for phone reservations and walk-ins. Everything starts at the yellow ＋ button.

5.1 The ＋ button (FAB)

Tap ＋ and three actions appear:

  • 1 📋 Add Booking — the full admin wizard, this chapter.
  • 2 🚶 Walk-in — opens the customer booking page in a new tab in walk-in mode, so the customer can fill in their own details at the counter (§5.7).
  • 3 🚫 Block Schedule — reserve time for lunch, leave, or maintenance (Chapter 8.1).
📷21-fab-open.pngMobile 390

  • 1 Add Booking
  • 2 Walk-in

5.2 The wizard at a glance

Add Booking walks through six steps shown in the step bar: Service → Schedule → Pet → Owner → Details → Summary. You can tap a previous step's name to jump back. Step one is simply choosing the service — Grooming, Pet Hotel, Daycare, or Studio.

5.3 Schedule — Grooming

  • 1 Pet size first — it sets the price of everything below.
  • 2 Package — Bath & Dry (30 min), Basic (1 hr), Premium (2 hrs), or Ala Carte (add-ons only; requires at least one add-on).
  • 3 Add-ons — priced items toggle on/off. On Premium, Face Trim shows included and can't be toggled. Dematting/Deshedding show Assessment — priced in store, not here.
  • 4 Groomer — pick a specific groomer or leave "Any available".
  • 5 Date → time slots — slots load for that date; taken slots are disabled. The availability already accounts for other bookings, blocked time, and "any available" overflow.

Size, date, drop-off and pick-up times. The base rate covers the first 3 hours; each extra hour adds a per-size hourly rate — the Summary will show e.g. Daycare (8h, +5 extra hrs). Toggle "Until open time" for open-ended pickup (base rate only; settle extras at checkout).

5.6 Pet, Owner, Details, Summary

Pet — name, species, sex, breed, size, age, temperament, medical notes, and the Vaccines on file Yes/No rows (dogs and cats have different vaccine lists).

Owner — before typing anything, search by phone or email 1. If the customer has booked before, tap their entry to pre-fill everything 2 — this keeps one customer = one record instead of duplicates.

Details — for hotel: feeding instructions, medications, emergency contact, vet info. For all services: the three waiver toggles (general terms, health declaration, media consent — confirm these verbally with the customer) and internal notes for staff.

Summary — review everything, then:

  • 1 Member code — type it and press Apply; a valid code shows ✓ and the discount line appears. Codes from the wrong branch, expired, or inactive memberships are rejected with a reason.
  • 2 Booking status — usually Confirmed; use Checked in if the pet is already in front of you.
  • 3 Payment status — if they pay now, set it and fill in method + reference #; a payment record is created automatically.
  • 4 Recorded by — your name, for the audit trail.
  • 5 Create booking.

If the owner's email is filled in, a confirmation email is sent immediately when you create the booking — admin bookings don't wait for payment.

5.7 Walk-in bookings

For walk-ins, prefer FAB → 🚶 Walk-in over the admin wizard: it opens the customer-facing booking flow in a new tab (secured by a one-time pass that only the admin app can create). Hand the customer the tablet/phone — they fill in their own pet, waivers, and contact details. Differences from an online booking: no convenience fee, no online payment — the final screen shows the amount to collect in-store, and the booking is tagged Walk-in. It appears on the Calendar instantly; collect payment and record it in the drawer (Chapter 7.4).

Ch 6

Changing a booking

Plans change — dates move, rooms swap, bookings get cancelled. All of it happens from the drawer.

6.1 The edit wizard

Open the booking's drawer and tap ✏ Edit booking. The same wizard from Chapter 5 opens, fully pre-filled, starting at the Schedule step. One deliberate restriction: the service type is locked — a hotel booking can't become a grooming booking 1. If the customer wants a different service, cancel this booking and create a new one.

📷29-edit-locked.pngDesktop 1280
Capture: Edit wizard on the Service step — other services dimmed, lock note visible.
  • 1 Dimmed service cards + "cannot be changed" note

6.2 Price-affecting edits

Change the dates, the cage, add-ons, pick-up hour, or the member code — the total recalculates automatically on the Summary step, and the bill lines in the drawer update when you save. If the customer already paid the old amount, the difference becomes a balance to collect (or refund) — see Chapter 7.4.

6.3 Moving a guest to another room / groomer

For a simple swap you don't need the full edit: use the Assign button in the drawer's Room/Groomer section (Chapter 4.3). The calendar updates immediately — the card moves to the new resource's color.

6.4 Cancelling

In the drawer's sticky footer, set booking status to cancelled and press Update. The booking disappears from the Check In page, frees its slot/room for new bookings, and appears greyed-out with strikethrough on the Calendar and Bookings list. It is never deleted — history stays intact.

6.5 Refunds

Refunds are recorded manually (money moves outside the system — cash drawer, bank, or e-wallet):

  1. Pay the customer back through the appropriate channel.
  2. Drawer → Payments → + Record → Type: Refund, with the amount and method.
  3. Set payment status to refunded (full refund) or leave as partially paid (partial), and press Update.

Ch 7

Front desk: check-in to check-out

The Check In page (🐾) is the front desk's daily queue: who's arriving, who's here, and who's leaving.

7.1 The three sections

  • 1 Awaiting Check-In — today's grooming/daycare/studio appointments plus hotel guests whose check-in date has arrived.
  • 2 In Progress — checked-in guests still being serviced or mid-stay.
  • 3 Due for Checkout — hotel guests whose check-out date is today; anything past its date is flagged ⚠ Overdue in red 4.

Each card shows the service, reference, pet, owner and time. The same search box works here too — and it searches all bookings, not just today's, so you can pull up any booking from the counter.

📷31-checkin-page.pngDesktop 1280
Capture: Check In page with all three sections populated, one overdue checkout if possible.
  • 1 Awaiting Check-In section
  • 2 In Progress
  • 3 Due for Checkout
  • 4 ⚠ Overdue pill

7.2 Checking a guest in

  1. Tap the guest's card — the drawer opens.
  2. Confirm identity, read the medical notes, check vaccines and waivers (especially play-park consent).
  3. Make sure a room/groomer is assigned — the app blocks check-in until it is.
  4. Fill in Check-in notes (next section).
  5. Sticky footer → status checked in → Update. The guest moves to "In Progress".

7.3 Check-in notes

In the drawer, the Check-in notes section (+ Add / Edit) opens a short condition report. The form adapts to the service:

  • Grooming — remarks + five Yes/No condition checks: vaccination complete, skin & coat, ears/nose/eyes, nails, joints.
  • Hotel — physical inspection notes, personal items left with the pet, add-on purchases.
  • Daycare — remarks + add-on purchases.
Fill this in with the owner present. A pre-existing skin condition noted at check-in protects both the pet and the staff later.
📷32-checkin-notes.pngMobile 390
Capture: grooming check-in notes form with the Yes/No rows, a couple toggled.
  • 1 Remarks field
  • 2 Yes/No condition rows

7.4 Collecting money — at any point

Whenever the customer pays — deposit at booking, balance at pickup, an add-on bought mid-stay — record it: drawer → Payments → + Record:

  • 1 Amount in pesos;
  • 2 Type — Downpayment, Balance, Add-on charge, or Refund;
  • 3 Method — Cash, Card, Bank transfer, or Online;
  • 4 Reference number (for card/transfer), optional notes, and your name under Recorded by.

Then update the payment status in the sticky footer to match reality (partially paid / paid).

📷33-record-payment.pngMobile 390
Capture: Record Payment panel, filled with a sample balance payment.
  • 1 Amount
  • 2 Type
  • 3 Method
  • 4 Reference + Recorded by

7.5 Checking out

  1. Open the guest from Due for Checkout (or In Progress).
  2. Check for extra charges:
    • Hotel late pick-up — every hour past 2 PM adds the hourly late fee; more than 6 hours past counts as an extra night; not picked up by 8 PM = an additional night.
    • Daycare extra hours — hours beyond the first 3 (or beyond what was prepaid) at the per-size hourly rate.
    • Any unpaid in-store add-ons.
  3. Collect the balance and record it (§7.4); set payment status to paid.
  4. Set booking status to completed → Update. The guest leaves the Check In page.

Ch 8

Managing the business

Blocking time off, membership records, and the rooms/groomers/studios themselves.

8.1 Blocking a schedule

Use a block whenever a resource shouldn't take bookings: a groomer's lunch or day off, a cage under repair, studio maintenance. Blocks remove those slots from the public booking site and the admin wizard simultaneously.

  1. ＋ FAB → 🚫 Block Schedule.
  2. Pick the resource type 1 — Hotel Room / Groomer / Studio — and which one 2.
  3. Tap one or more dates on the mini calendar 3 (the count confirms how many are selected).
  4. Set start/end time 4, write a short reason ("Lunch", "On leave"), and save.

8.2 Members

The Members page does two jobs:

Validate in-store 1 — when a customer claims a membership, enter their code (and pet name). You'll get one of: ✓ valid (with tier, coverage and expiry), pet name doesn't match, expired (with the date), or not found / inactive. Two tiers exist: Standard (one branch only) and Passport (all branches) — a Standard code from the other branch will be rejected at booking time.

CSV upload 2 — membership records are maintained as a spreadsheet and uploaded here. The file must have exactly these headers: Membership ID, Pet Name, Valid Until Date, Branch. Existing codes are updated, new codes added — the result line tells you how many of each.

📷36-members.pngDesktop 1280

  • 1 Validate card with result
  • 2 CSV upload card

8.3 Inventory — rooms, groomers, studios

The Inventory page (📦) manages the resources everything else depends on. Three tabs: Rooms · Groomers · Studios 1, each listing the branch's active items with their color dot 2.

  • Add — the dashed button opens the form: name, color (this becomes the calendar color), and for rooms: cage type, pet type, and allowed pet sizes.
  • Edit — tap an item. Rooms can be locked (with a reason) to stop new bookings; groomers/studios can be marked unavailable (with a reason) for longer absences. For a single day off, prefer a block (§8.1).
  • Remove — retires the item: it vanishes from every dropdown and the public site, but old bookings keep showing its name. Nothing is lost.

App B

Pricing cheatsheet

How each service is priced. Exact peso amounts live in the pricing table and appear automatically in the wizard — this is the logic.

ServiceHow the price is built
GroomingPackage price by pet size (Bath & Dry / Basic / Premium; Ala Carte has no base) + add-ons. Face Trim is size-priced and free with Premium. Dematting & Deshedding are assessment-priced in store.
HotelNightly rate by cage type — Small/Medium/Large Cage, Cat Cabin, Cat Villa — × day type: Fri/Sat/Sun nights bill at the weekend rate, Mon–Thu at the weekday rate. + late pick-up: per-hour fee after 2 PM; >6 h late = extra night; not collected by 8 PM = extra night.
DaycareBase rate by pet size covers the first 3 hours; each additional hour adds a per-size hourly rate. Open-time = base now, extras settled at pickup.
StudioSession priced at the studio; settled at check-out.
Member discountPercentage off the subtotal — currently 10% grooming, 20% hotel, 20% daycare. Standard = home branch only; Passport = both branches.
Convenience feeOnline card/e-wallet bookings only. Never added to admin or walk-in bookings.

Shortcuts & tips

ActionEffect
EscCloses the top-most open panel — wizard, block panel, filter sheet, drawer, month picker — one layer at a time.
RRefreshes the Calendar / Bookings data. Ignored while you're typing in a field or a panel is open.
Tap outside a panelCloses it (same as Esc).
Tap a date group headerCollapses / expands that day in the Bookings list.
  • Search beats scrolling — on Bookings and Check In, two characters of the pet's name is usually faster than hunting through the list.
  • Always search for the owner first when creating bookings — duplicated owners fragment a customer's history.
  • Record payments the moment they happen. The drawer's payment list is the branch's money trail.
  • Use blocks for hours, Inventory lock/unavailable for long absences.

Troubleshooting / FAQ

SymptomCheck, in order
"A booking is missing"① Correct branch selected? ② Calendar on the right date? ③ A service tab or resource filter active? (Tap All / clear the filter.) ④ Was it cancelled? Search its ref on the Bookings page.
An online booking shows pending / unpaidThe customer hasn't completed payment. They have ~15 minutes; if payment fails or lapses, the system auto-cancels and frees the slot. No action needed.
A grooming time slot is greyed out but the groomer "looks free"The slot may be consumed by an "Any available" booking that hasn't been assigned yet, or by a block. Check the calendar's All view and any hatched blocks at that hour.
Can't check a guest inAssign a room/groomer first — check-in is blocked while the booking is Unassigned.
Data looks stalePress R, or switch away from the tab and back. The page also self-refreshes every minute.
"Not authorized" after Google sign-inYour Google account isn't on the admin list — ask a manager to add it, then sign in again.
A member code is rejectedRead the message: wrong branch (Standard codes are branch-locked), expired, inactive, or a typo. Verify on the Members page.
Customer asks for an emailed receiptConfirmation emails go out automatically — at payment for online bookings, immediately for admin-created ones — if an email address is on the booking. Check the Owner section; add the email via Edit and re-save if missing.
