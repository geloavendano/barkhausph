Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about our services, booking, and caring for your pet at Barkhaus.

General

What is Barkhaus?

Barkhaus is a pet care destination offering dog hotel, daycare, grooming, and play services in a safe, supervised, and comfortable environment. It is designed to give pet parents peace of mind while their dogs enjoy a happy, enriching experience.

Who is Barkhaus for?

Barkhaus is ideal for dog parents who need reliable pet boarding, a safe place for daytime care, or professional grooming services. It is especially helpful for busy owners, frequent travelers, and pets who enjoy socialization and structured play.

Do I need to book in advance or can I walk-in?

Advanced booking is highly recommended, especially during weekends, holidays, and peak travel seasons. This helps ensure slot availability and allows the team to prepare for your dog's visit. You may make your booking through our booking portal.

What are the accepted payment options?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, and JCB for Debit and Credit Cards, and GCash, Maya, and QR PH for e-wallet and bank transfers.

Pet Hotel & Cat Boarding

What should I bring when checking in my dog or boarding my cat?

Bring your pet's food, any necessary medication, vaccination records if required, and comfort items like a familiar blanket or toy. Keeping your pet's routine as familiar as possible helps them settle in more easily.

How do you keep boarded pets safe?

Safety is maintained through expert 24/7 care, supervised playgroup matching, clean facilities, and proper feeding and handling protocols. Your pets are monitored closely to reduce stress and prevent unnecessary conflicts or accidents. We also provide daily photo updates so you can rest assured they are given the utmost care and attention.

Will my pet get exercise during their stay?

Yes, pets receive daily movement and supervised activity based on their energy level, temperament, and with owners' consent. We value the need for your pets to have their days as balanced as possible while under our care and supervision.

Daycare

Is daycare good for social pets?

Yes, daycare can be a great option for pets that enjoy human interaction, playtime, and structured activity. Feel free to bring them over and have them interact with our staff and the other pets!

How do you decide if a dog is fit for daycare?

A responsible daycare evaluates temperament, social behavior, health status, and comfort level around other dogs. The goal is to make sure each pet can participate safely and enjoyably.

What if my dog is shy or nervous?

Shy dogs can still benefit from daycare if they are introduced slowly and placed in an environment that respects their pace. Not every dog needs the same play style, and a good facility should adjust care accordingly.

Grooming

How often should my dog be groomed?

Grooming frequency depends on coat type, activity level, and lifestyle, but most dogs benefit from regular grooming every few weeks to a few months. Consistent grooming helps maintain coat health, skin comfort, and hygiene.

What is included in a grooming session?

A grooming session may include shampoo, blow-dry, brush out, plus essential care like teeth brushing, paw pad cleaning, ear cleaning, and more depending on the package and any add-ons selected. The exact service will always be explained before the appointment.

My dog is anxious during grooming. Can you still groom them?

Many dogs are nervous at first, and our grooming team have the experience and capability to treat and handle your pets lovingly and carefully.

Safety & Health

Do dogs need vaccinations before visiting?

Yes, most pet care facilities require updated vaccinations to help protect all dogs in the facility. This is especially important in shared environments like daycare, hotel, and boarding.

What happens if my dog has special needs?

Special needs should always be discussed before booking so the team can assess whether the dog's care requirements can be properly supported. Clear communication helps ensure the safest and most comfortable experience.

Still have questions?

Our team is happy to help. Visit any Barkhaus branch or reach out to us directly — we'll make sure you and your pet feel taken care of.
